The Legends of Time: Rise of the Exiles

Welcome to Season 1. Centuries have passed since the Exiles forged the NeoCube. Now, three kingdoms—Wealth, Power, and Wisdom—defy the oppressive rule of an enigmatic figure. Will you rise as a conqueror?

Introduction:

Centuries have passed since the first exiles set foot in the mysterious lands of time. They, the pioneers who forged the NeoCube, a sacred artifact capable of bending reality itself, unknowingly unleashed a force that would shape the fate of kingdoms.

With its discovery, an enigmatic figure emerged, wielding the NeoCube's power to control these lands and dictate the rise and fall of mighty realms. Yet, amidst this dominion, a glimmer of resistance remains. Three kingdoms, resolute in their defiance, defy the oppressive rule.

  • The Kingdom of Wealth: Where opulence and fortune thrive.
  • The Kingdom of Power: A bastion of strength and unyielding might.
  • The Kingdom of Wisdom: Where knowledge and intellect illuminate the path ahead.

But their dominion is fragile, their thrones vacant, their rulers lost to the annals of history. Now, the remnants of noble bloodlines have splintered, each vying to forge their own kingdoms, their own legacies.

As chaos consumes the realm, the lone Minister, unwavering in loyalty to the fallen King, watches from the Throne Room. Yet, a shadow looms from the underworld—an Overlord ascends.

It falls to you, brave Exile, to gather your loyal subjects and claim your birthright as the Supreme King of the Land. Will you rise as a conqueror or succumb to the tempest?

Patch Notes & Known Bugs

We've squashed major bugs including the "missing legs" glitch and "deadly laser sun," though some issues with moon size and entity textures persist.

Resolved Issues

  • Resolved an issue where players spawned without legs.
  • Addressed a bug where shields failed to provide protection while sneaking.
  • Corrected a bug preventing saturation restoration when eating while flying.
  • Fixed Elytra activation with jump button causing unintended space propulsion.
  • Eliminated a bug causing involuntary teleportation to coordinates -1287 89 324.
  • Fixed inventory wipes upon consuming food.
  • Ender pearls no longer cause fatal harm on ocean contact.
  • NPC generation adjusted: NPCs no longer spawn in farming areas.

Known Bugs

  • Certain entities render without proper textures.
  • Cows drop "Bucket of M!lk" instead of "Bucket of Milk".
  • The sun is still a deadly laser.
  • The moon appears larger than it should.
  • Impossible to disengage Elytra in creative mode.
  • Menu screens and UI are glitched.
  • Excessive flowers spawning on mountains.

New Gear: Gilded Items

A new tier of equipment has arrived. Craft Gilded Netherite for superior durability and protection. Includes the all-new Mace weapon class!

How to Craft

  1. Smelt: Burn Gilded Blackstone in a furnace or blast furnace to get Melted Gilded Blackstone.
  2. Combine: Craft Gilded Netherite Ingots.
  3. Apply: Combine directly with Netherite equipment in a crafting table.

Bonus: 1+ piece of armor makes piglins ignore you. Full set grants +4 Extra Health.

New Weapon: The Mace

Breaks cobwebs and bamboo instantly. Features a "Bash" attack (Right Click/Hold) that deals heavy durability damage to opponent armor.

  • Gilded Mace: 50 durability damage per bash.
  • Netherite Mace: 42 durability damage per bash.
  • Diamond Mace: 35 durability damage per bash.

World Update: Biomania

Explore the landscape with brand new biomes including the Cherry Blossom Grove and Shattered Taiga. New trees, plants, and blocks await.

New Biomes

  • Cherry Blossom Grove: Full of pink petals, Cherry trees, and atmosphere fog.
  • Shattered Taiga: A chaotic, rugged landscape with massive cliffs.
  • Dunes: Vast sandy expanses with new "Dune Grass" vegetation.
  • Rainforest: Dense, lush, and dark forests.
  • Shrubland & Pumpkin Patch: New variations for gathering resources.

New Blocks & Particles

Added Cherry Sapling, Cherry Stairs, Dune Grass, Fluffy Cattail, and Sand Dust Particles for atmosphere.

New Foods & Farming

A massive culinary update with 70+ new foods, 40+ new crops, and new kitchen machinery like blenders and ovens.

Crops & Trees

Trade with the Wandering Farmer to get seeds. Grow Maple (syrup!), Cherry, Palm (coconuts), Cinnamon, Orange, Lemon, Mango, and Peach trees.

New Machinery

  • Ovens: Cook food using cooking oil instead of coal.
  • Mill: Grind grains and fruits.
  • Presser: Make juice without fuel.
  • Brewer & Blender: Create sodas and smoothies.

New Dishes

Craft Kabobs (roast on campfire), Pies, Cakes, and various fruit dishes. Find the Aimless Baker roaming the world to buy supplies!
